How TaskFlow works

A simple tool for tracking tasks, managing teams and organizing projects.

1

Create an account

Register and set up your profile. Choose your position and start working.

2

Create or join a team

Build your team or join an existing one. Collaborate with colleagues on shared goals.

3

Set up a project

Create a project and assign teams to it. Keep all related work in one place.

4

Track tasks

Create tasks, assign them to team members, set deadlines and track progress.

What you can do
  • ✅ Create and assign tasks with priorities and deadlines
  • ✅ Mark tasks as completed
  • ✅ Filter tasks by status or assignee
  • ✅ Create and manage teams
  • ✅ Create projects and attach teams to them
  • ✅ View all workers and their completed tasks
  • ✅ Update your profile and position
  • ✅ Assign yourself to any task
